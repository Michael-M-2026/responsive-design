Yellow vs Green

Yellow Party's Banana Peel today, slipped up by incidentally declaring their distate for the Green Party's stance on the colorwheel. Mr. Peel stated that he had no quarrel with Green Party's Mr. Pea, but "something about Ms. Pea" rubbed him off the wrong way. Considering this an attack on Ms. Pea's character, Mr. Pea threw down their pod and challenged Mr. Peel to a dual. The two parties had previously not shown any sign of animosity, but skeptics consider Ms. Pea's presence a factor in Mr. Pea's altered attitude today. The battle is scheduled to take place at Plate stadium at Dinner time. The Yellow Party and Green Party are offering their respective members free transportation, via carpools to attend the gathering. More information is avaliable on our website.

RED PARTY'S TOMATO FOUND DEAD

Red party's infamous Tomato was found dead in his apartment yesterday. He was discovered by the now widowed Ms. Tomato who, upon returning home, found Tomato turned into tomato-paste in their bath-tub. This absolutely morbid scene has law enforcement scrambling, trying to uncover any leads as soon as possible. This tragedy comes amongst a time of great turmoil amongst the kitchen; with tensions so high, the Silverware presume the Pasta mafia might be responsible for the crime. "The Ronnis and Elbows never liked Tomato" the Silverware chief exclaimed. "If Tomato had a target on his back then the Pasta Mafia would be the first ones to shoot." While the evidence at the crime scene are little to none, the violent history between the Red Party and The Pasta mafia might provide a feasible offense in court. Ms. Tomato claims that she will get justice no matter the cost. "I loved Tomato, and those damn Pastas took him from me. You won't see the end of me."

Tomato's funeral will be held a week from now. The Red Party has refused to make a statement regarding their representative. His legacy is carried on by his wife, and three children. He was only 24.
